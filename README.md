# Northbridge Critical Systems

Technical risk, reliability and delivery advisory for fintech, Web3, SaaS, AI, embedded-to-cloud and regulated teams.

## Core offer

**Critical Systems Risk Audit** maps failure modes across architecture, platform, CI/CD, QA strategy, observability, incident readiness, payment flows, AI workflows and compliance evidence.

## Best fit

- FinTech / payment products
- Web3 wallets, exchanges and infrastructure
- B2B SaaS under production or compliance pressure
- AI products moving from prototype to production
- Embedded-to-cloud / IoT / industrial systems
- Regulated enterprise environments

## Output

- Top technical and operational risks
- Release-readiness and observability gaps
- Risk-prioritized remediation backlog
- 30/60/90 roadmap
- Executive summary for CTOs, founders and stakeholders
