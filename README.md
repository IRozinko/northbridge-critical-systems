# NorthBridge Critical Systems

Technical-risk, reliability and delivery advisory for systems where failure is expensive.

NorthBridge is a public proof and advisory shell around one operating idea: complex systems rarely fail in one isolated layer. They fail across architecture, infrastructure, CI/CD, QA strategy, observability, incident response, data flows, payments, AI workflows, compliance evidence and unclear ownership.

Website: https://ivan-rozinko.vercel.app  
Core offer: https://ivan-rozinko.vercel.app/services/critical-systems-risk-audit

## Core offer

**Critical Systems Risk Audit** maps the highest technical and operational risks across architecture, platform, CI/CD, QA strategy, observability, incident readiness, payment flows, AI workflows and compliance evidence.

The output is meant for decision-making: what to fix now, what to monitor, what to document, what to automate and what to defer safely.

## Who this is for

- CTOs, founders and engineering leaders who need an independent technical-risk view
- FinTech, Web3, SaaS, AI and regulated product teams
- Teams preparing a major release, migration, audit, funding diligence or scale step
- Platforms with payments, wallets, partner flows, transaction states or sensitive data
- Engineering organizations that need stronger release confidence and operational readiness

## What I review

- Architecture and ownership boundaries
- CI/CD, release gates and rollback readiness
- QA strategy, automation coverage and regression risk
- Platform reliability, cloud infrastructure and scaling paths
- Observability, alerting, incident response and runbooks
- Security controls and compliance evidence
- Payment, wallet, transaction and money-flow risks
- AI workflow, LLM agent and automation risks
- Team process, delivery predictability and decision ownership

## Deliverables

- Risk map of the highest technical and operational risks
- Top failure modes with business impact and missing controls
- Evidence review across documentation, monitoring, tests and release process
- 30/60/90 remediation roadmap
- Executive summary for founders, CTOs and stakeholders
