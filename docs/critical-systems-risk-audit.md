# Critical Systems Risk Audit

## Assessment areas

1. Architecture and ownership boundaries
2. CI/CD and release gates
3. QA strategy and automation coverage
4. Platform reliability and cloud infrastructure
5. Observability and incident readiness
6. Security and compliance evidence
7. Payment / transaction / money-flow risks
8. AI agent / LLM workflow risks
9. Embedded-to-cloud boundary risks
10. Team process and delivery risk

## Deliverables

- Risk map
- Failure-mode notes
- Prioritized remediation backlog
- 30/60/90 action plan
- Executive summary

## Scoring

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 1 | Controlled and documented |
| 2 | Mostly controlled, minor gaps |
| 3 | Known risk, partial controls |
| 4 | High risk, weak controls |
| 5 | Critical risk, no reliable controls |
